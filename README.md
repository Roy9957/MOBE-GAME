# MOBE: Multiplayer Online Battle Experience

## Overview
MOBE is a multiplayer space-fighting game where you control a spaceship to battle against enemies.  
The game provides an exciting experience through multiplayer mode, solo training mode, and captivating space battle mechanics.

## Features
- **Real-time Multiplayer Battles**: Engage in space battles with friends or players from around the globe.
- **Solo Training Mode**: Play solo training mode to enhance your skills.
- **Leaderboard**: View the top players on the leaderboard.
